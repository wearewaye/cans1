I’ll spare you the me talking in the 3rd person,

My name is Giacomo Jay Ganduglia and I’m a Creative Director and Visual Artist based in London.

As a Visual Artist, I always believed that being a specialist wasn't something for me, I always loved to explore new things, new software, techniques and art styles. I created a solid knowledge of Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, working in both photography and illustration, then Premiere Pro, Final Cut and After Effects, and, lately, Blender. I'm still learning and developing different workflows for different projects, using both 3d, 2d and real-life (video and still). I won't stop learning, failing and starting again and most of all trying to get the best result having fun in the process. Some refer at people like me as multipotentialite I just love to think no one ever stops learning, being curious is the key.

 What a “ViSuAL aRtIsT” do? 

Being a generalist is basically the natural consequences of not being able to choose one thing and keeping on learning and specializing in new interesting stuff. I started as a graffiti and comicbook artist, and then moved to new things starting with graphic design in 2005. Having a comprehensive knowledge of the different creative processes, from pre to post production, allows me to plan and develop creatively the workflow in the different steps.
